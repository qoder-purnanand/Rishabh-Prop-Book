Settings & Configuration
Master data source configuration · All calculations update in real-time
Setting 1 — Master Data Source
Net Worth:
—
ℹ Helper: Networth Inputs is the established working sheet. Sheet1 is a newer format with an On/Off toggle. These two sheets currently have different values for the same line items.
Excel Source: Networth Inputs sheet Col E (assets, rows 3+) minus Col M (debt, rows 3+). OR Sheet1 rows 3+ Col F (values) filtered by Col H (status).
Setting 2 — Sheet1 On/Off Toggle
Not applicable — Networth Inputs has no On/Off toggle.
Active / Included Items:
—
ℹ Helper: Off rows in Sheet1 include locked FDs, unrealised P&L not yet confirmed, and deposits marked as excluded.
Excel Source: Sheet1 Col H ("On/Off" status). When "On items only": filter for rows where Col H = "On". When "All rows": include all rows.
Setting 3 — Strategy Exposure Source
Total PSAR Notional:
—
ℹ Helper: The Strategy Allocation sheet has its own internal formulas that reference Networth Inputs. If your master source (Setting 1) is Sheet1, pulling directly from Strategy Allocation may create inconsistency.
Excel Source: Strategy Allocation sheet rows 4+ Col F (Net Exposure) for direct use. OR recompute using Col D (Position Sizing) × master liquidity from Setting 1.
Setting 4 — Holdings P&L Source
Total Holdings Value:
—
ℹ Helper: Equity Holdings and Mutual Funds sheets have stock-level detail including LTP, quantity, buy price, and P&L per position. Networth Inputs only has category-level totals.
Excel Source: Equity Holdings sheet rows 4+ Col K (Current Value) and Col L (P&L). Mutual Funds sheet rows 4+ Col L (Current Value) and Col M (P&L). OR Networth Inputs sheet filtered categories for aggregated totals.
